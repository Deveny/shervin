I am a Belgian architect/ creator with Iranian roots who is based in Ghent. Through my profession I convert my personal chaos into structural order. In this way I try to find intrinsic serenity and I always use my intuition as a starting point. I try to transform my state of mind into a place that is transcended by materiality and thus forms an open narrative. I draw from daily compositions, texts, visual arts, and above all from dialogues with the other. At the moment I work as an architect with Jan De Vylder and Inge Vinck and I teach Mixed Media and Design at LUCA School of Arts in Ghent and Brussels.
